UNIVERSITY_OVERVIEW
Vignan’s Foundation for Science, Technology and Research (VFSTR) is a deemed university located in Vadlamudi, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh, India. The university provides quality education in engineering, science, management, agriculture, pharmacy, and law programs. The campus spans around 72 acres and focuses on research, innovation, and skill development.

UNIVERSITY_FACULTY_AND_INFRASTRUCTURE
The university has dedicated faculty members, many from premier institutions such as IITs and NITs. The campus includes modern laboratories, research centers, incubation centers, and innovation hubs that support academic excellence and research activities.

UNIVERSITY_RESEARCH_AND_LEARNING
Vignan University promotes research and innovation through centers of excellence, laboratories, and industry collaborations. Students have access to digital learning platforms, e-journals, research resources, and high-speed internet facilities.

ADMISSION_PROGRAM_INFORMATION
Vignan University offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, management, agriculture, pharmacy, and law disciplines.

UNDERGRADUATE_PROGRAMS
Undergraduate programs include B.Tech, BBA, BCA, B.Sc, B.Sc (Hons) Agriculture, B.Pharmacy, Pharm-D, BA LL.B (Hons), and BBA LL.B (Hons).

BTECH_SPECIALIZATIONS
B.Tech specializations include Computer Science and Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Data Science, Internet of Things, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Agricultural Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Robotics and Automation, Textile Technology, Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, and Bioinformatics.

POSTGRADUATE_PROGRAMS
Postgraduate programs include MBA, MBA Business Analytics, MCA, M.Tech, M.Sc programs, M.Pharmacy, and PhD programs in various disciplines.

ADMISSION_ENTRANCE_EXAMS
Admissions are conducted through entrance examinations such as V-SAT (Vignan Scholastic Aptitude Test), EAPCET, JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET for applicable programs.

ADMISSION_ELIGIBILITY
Students applying for undergraduate programs must complete Intermediate or 10+2 education with appropriate subject combinations such as MPC or BiPC depending on the course.

ADMISSION_MINIMUM_MARKS
Candidates typically require a minimum of 60 percent aggregate marks in the qualifying examination for admission. Higher marks improve eligibility for scholarships.

ADMISSION_BIPC_ELIGIBILITY
BiPC students are eligible to apply for certain programs including biotechnology, pharmacy, and some interdisciplinary engineering programs.

ADMISSION_APPLICATION_PROCESS
Students can apply through the official admissions portal by registering online, filling out the application form, uploading required documents, and paying the application fee.

ADMISSION_APPLICATION_PORTAL
Students can apply online through the admissions portal at https://admissions.vignan.ac.in
.

APPLICATION_TRACKING
Applicants can track their admission status, download entrance examination hall tickets, and receive updates through the online admissions portal.

ACADEMIC_SUPPORT_REGISTRATION
Students register for courses based on their semester curriculum through the academic system after admission.

ACADEMIC_PROJECT_BASED_CURRICULUM
Vignan follows a project-based curriculum where students work on practical projects each semester starting from the second year to improve industry readiness.

ACADEMIC_CREDIT_SYSTEM
The university follows a credit-based academic system where students earn credits through core subjects, electives, laboratories, internships, and projects.

ACADEMIC_MINOR_PROGRAMS
Students can pursue interdisciplinary minor courses in management, humanities, sciences, and civil services preparation.

ACADEMIC_CALENDAR_STRUCTURE
The academic year follows a semester system including lectures, laboratory sessions, examinations, internships, workshops, seminars, and project evaluations.

ACADEMIC_EVENTS
The university organizes national-level events such as Srujanankura youth festival which includes technical, sports, cultural, and literary competitions.

FEE_STRUCTURE_BTECH
The approximate tuition fee for B.Tech CSE related programs under Category A admission is around ₹2,80,000 per year.

FEE_STRUCTURE_BTECH_CATEGORY_B
Under Category B admission the B.Tech CSE related programs may have tuition fees around ₹3,80,000 per year.

FEE_STRUCTURE_OTHER_PROGRAMS
Other programs such as BBA, BCA, MBA, and M.Tech have tuition fees ranging approximately between ₹80,000 and ₹3,00,000 per year depending on the course.

ADMISSION_FEES
Admission fees generally range between ₹10,000 and ₹20,000 depending on the program selected.

SCHOLARSHIP_CRITERIA
Vignan University offers merit-based scholarships based on performance in entrance exams such as V-SAT, JEE Main, EAPCET, NEET, and marks obtained in Intermediate examinations.

SCHOLARSHIP_TYPES
Scholarships may provide 75 percent, 50 percent, 25 percent, or 10 percent tuition fee reduction depending on merit and rank.

SCHOLARSHIP_CONTINUATION_RULE
Students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 7.0 each academic year to continue receiving scholarship benefits.

EDUCATION_LOAN_SUPPORT
Students can apply for education loans through nationalized banks, and the university provides necessary admission and fee documents for loan processing.

HOSTEL_FACILITIES
Vignan University provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls within the campus.

HOSTEL_ROOM_TYPES
Hostel accommodation includes AC and Non-AC room options for students.

HOSTEL_SERVICES
Hostel facilities include Wi-Fi connectivity, mess services, study rooms, security, and basic living amenities.

HOSTEL_FEES_AC
The approximate hostel fee for AC rooms is around ₹1,45,000 per year.

HOSTEL_FEES_NON_AC
The approximate hostel fee for non-AC rooms is around ₹1,15,000 per year.

TRANSPORT_SERVICES
The university operates bus transportation services connecting nearby cities and towns.

TRANSPORT_LOCATIONS
Transportation facilities are available for students commuting from Guntur, Vijayawada, Tenali, Nellore, Vizag, Hyderabad, and Warangal.

CAMPUS_INFRASTRUCTURE
The campus includes research laboratories, centers of excellence, incubation centers, sports facilities, libraries with digital journals, and student activity clubs.

LABORATORIES_AND_RESEARCH
More than 88 laboratories and multiple research centers support practical learning, innovation, and technical research activities.

MENTAL_HEALTH_COUNSELING
The university provides psychological counseling services for students through experienced counselors and faculty mentors.

MENTAL_HEALTH_SUPPORT
Students can receive support for academic stress, personal challenges, emotional wellbeing, and career guidance.

STRESS_MANAGEMENT_PROGRAMS
Stress management resources include yoga programs, meditation sessions, personality development training, life skills workshops, and extracurricular activities.

STUDENT_CLUBS
Students can participate in clubs related to music, drama, photography, environment, sports, and cultural activities.

PLACEMENT_RECORD
Vignan University maintains strong placement performance with approximately 85 to 90 percent of students securing job placements each year.

PLACEMENT_TRAINING
Students receive placement training including communication skills, technical training, mock interviews, aptitude preparation, and internship opportunities from early years of study.

COMPANIES_RECRUITING
Major companies recruiting students include TCS, Accenture, Cognizant, Wipro, and HCL.

PLACEMENT_SALARY
The highest salary packages have reached approximately ₹44 lakh per annum with internship stipends up to ₹80,000.
