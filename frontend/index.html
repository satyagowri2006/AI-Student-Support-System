Student Mental Health & Counseling

At Vignan University, student well-being is a top priority. We offer comprehensive mental health and counseling services designed to support students in managing academic pressure, emotional well-being, and personal challenges. Our certified counselors and mentors are committed to helping students achieve a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Our Counseling Services

  • ✔ Confidential one-on-one counseling with licensed professionals
  • ✔ Group therapy sessions and peer support initiatives
  • ✔ Workshops on stress, anxiety, and emotional resilience
  • ✔ Academic and exam-related stress management guidance
  • ✔ Career counseling and personal growth development
