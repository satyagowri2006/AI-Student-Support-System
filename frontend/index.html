About Vignan University

Vignan’s Foundation for Science, Technology and Research (VFSTR) is a premier educational institution known for academic excellence, research innovation, and strong industry collaboration. The university offers modern laboratories, centres of excellence, and industry-driven curriculum to prepare students for successful careers. With world-class infrastructure, experienced faculty, and excellent placement records, Vignan continues to empower students with knowledge, skills, and leadership qualities.
